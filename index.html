Dan Weisz

Assistant Professor of Astronomy at UC Berkeley
I study near-field cosmology, stars, dark matter, and the smallest galaxies in the Universe. I have received an Alfred P. Sloan Fellowship, an Alexander von Humboldt Fellowship, and the 2019 Newton Lacy Pierce Prize from the American Astronomical Society. I will be among the first users of the James Webb Space Telescope.
